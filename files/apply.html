Application Procedure

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one monthâ€™s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

You must complete out an application form to apply for admission. This form only needs to be filled out once. You may fill out the Application for Admission online. Alternatively, you may print out and return the PDF Version in person. Upon admission, see the Enroll page for information on next steps.
